Concept

What is Motion UI?

Motion UI is the practice of adding subtle, purposeful animation to user interfaces. Instead of static screens, elements move, fade, scale or slide to give visual feedback and make the experience more natural.

Good motion does not decorate — it communicates. It tells the user what changed, what to look at next, and how parts of the interface connect.

Pros & Cons

Benefits and trade-offs

Advantages

  • Guides the user's attention naturally
  • Provides instant visual feedback
  • Makes transitions feel smooth and logical
  • Improves perceived performance

Limitations

  • Too much motion can distract users
  • May affect accessibility (motion sickness)
  • Heavy animations can hurt performance
  • Requires careful design decisions
